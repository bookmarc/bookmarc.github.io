AstroSlot 🛰️

AstroSlot is your personal mission control for the night sky.

Never miss a clear window to the cosmos again. AstroSlot syncs real-time data from NASA, SpaceX, NOAA Space Weather, and WeatherKit to surface upcoming celestial events tailored to your exact location — ISS passes, Starlink train sightings, solar flares, meteor showers, aurora forecasts, and near-Earth asteroid approaches.

Beat Stride 2 🏃

BeatStride: Your Heart & Step Efficiency Tracker

Take control of your fitness journey with BeatStride. By analyzing your heart rate and steps, our app gives you a unique metric—the Heart Efficiency Index—to help you understand your fitness level in a new way.

Key Features:

  • Heart Efficiency Index: Get a single, easy-to-understand score that reveals your heart's efficiency.
  • Daily & Monthly Charts: View your progress over time with intuitive, color-coded heat maps and charts.
  • Track Your Progress: See how your efficiency improves with every step you take.
  • Seamless Integration: Syncs effortlessly with Apple Health to securely access your data.

SatietyLog 🍽️

SatietyLog is your personal guide to mindful eating after surgery.

Designed with bariatric and post-surgical patients in mind, SatietyLog makes it easy to track your hunger and fullness levels throughout the day, helping you build healthier eating habits and stay on track with your recovery goals.

Key Features:

  • Satiety Logging: Quickly log your hunger and fullness before and after every meal.
  • Meal Tracking: Record what you eat and how your body responds over time.
  • Progress Insights: Visualize trends and patterns in your eating habits with intuitive charts.
  • Privacy-First: All your data stays on your device — no accounts required.
